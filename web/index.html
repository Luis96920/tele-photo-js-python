Welcome to TelePhoto

An image-based game of Telephone starting with YOU and passing through two generative AI models.

The first AI model generates a caption for your image.
The second AI model generates an image for that caption.
How far will this game of TelePhoto take your image?

Files uploaded to this website are stored for a maximum of 1 day and are automatically deleted after that time.
